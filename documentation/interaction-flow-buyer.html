Interaction Flow for Buyer

Use case: Marty is looking for a copy of Sol Ring to put in his deck, and would like to find it locally.

Precondition: Marty is logged in on Burque MTG Finder on his phone.

Postcondition: Marty starts arranging to trade his arcane signet for a sol ring with someone located locally.

Interaction Flow:

  • Marty clicks on the menu link in the menu bar to take him to the search page.
  • The website redirects him to a page with a search bar.
  • Marty types in “sol ring” into the search bar and hits search.
  • The site pulls up results for sol ring postings from around Albuquerque
  • Marty clicks on the first listing
  • The website opens to a page with more info about that listing, including who is trading it and what they want for it, and a picture of the card he is looking for, and an option to message the seller.
  • Marty clicks on the message button, types a message into the box, and hits send
  • The website shows that Marty has sent a message to the seller.
  • Marty waits to hear back from the seller and clicks on his inbox
  • The website shows Marty his inbox, and previous messages he has received, and any new messages he gets
