User Case for Seller

    George wants to sell some cards he is not using.

Stories Seller

As a seller, I want to be able to upload a list of cards I want to sell.

Interaction Flow

  • George types the url for the site.
  • the site returns to the home page.
  • George logins in with his information. Then hits the submit button.
  • The site reloads to the profile page.
  • George clicks on his sell list.
  • The site redirects to the listing page.
  • George clicks on card image to upload picture of his card to upload.
  • The page shows the picture he selected.
  • George adds the asking price and description.
  • George clicks on the post listing button.
  • The site reloads to George's profile page.
  • George sees that his card is uploaded to his listing on his profile.
  • George clicks the logout button
