Seller Persona

Name: George Kephart

Technology:

  • George has a macbook pro 13'3 inch laptop. Iphone 14 pro max in pink.
  • George is very confident in his abilities to use technology.

Profession:

  • George is a professional standard magic player.
  • He travels to various tournaments to compete.
  • Because the cards in standard are constantly changing, George has a stockpile of cards he cannot use.

Attitudes and Behaviors:

  • George is constantly traveling for tournaments but always comes back to Albuquerque NM, when he is done.
  • Since he is well adapt in technology he would like to be able to sell/trade easily online.
  • George also likes to trade cards when he can inorder to build a new deck.
  • He is always looking for a new trading app.

Frustration and Needs:

  • George has so many unused cards he does not know what to do with them.
  • He needs an easy way to sell them when he is back in town.
  • He is looking to maybe trade some of them for the newer cards he needs.

Goals:

  • George would like to either sel or trade to build a new standard deck.
  • He would like to easily be able to browse for trades.
  • George also doesn't mind selling for cash.
